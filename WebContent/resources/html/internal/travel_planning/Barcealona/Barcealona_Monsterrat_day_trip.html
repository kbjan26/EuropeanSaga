Day Trip to Monsterrat.

Monsterrat is one of the most sought destinations in Barcealona and when you are in the city this is one place that is to be missed.You will defintely require one whole day to plan for Monsterrat

  1. The whole package to Monsterrat including the train,free metro ride with funicular or cable car ride is 29.50 Euros
  2. Ensure you buy this ticket a day before you start the travel since you can make use of 2 free metro rides to and from your accomodation.It will have 2.15 Euro one way.
  3. If you are taking cable car ride then you must get down at Aeri de Montserrat station.
  4. If you are taking train ride then you must get down at Monistrol station that comes after Aeri de Montserrat station.
  5. Be prepared for a long hike along the mountains of Monsterrat and the views are simply spectacular.
  6. Try the local cheese made of goat milk which I personally liked with honey toppings.
  7. Do not miss on the black choir at the monastery that happens twice a day 12.00 PM and another at 18.45 PM. It is one of the must watch events when you are here.

What does the ticket package include?.

  1. 2 free metro rides ( can be used to and from accomodation).
  2. Round trip train to Monsterrat station from Espanya.
  3. Unlimited use of funicular trains.
  4. Ride with either cable car or railway train to the top.Both are unique in their own way with beautiful views.
  5. Entry to the museum.

Do not buy separate Renfe R2 train ticke to head to airport.You could leverage one of the rides of T-10 to reach the city.
