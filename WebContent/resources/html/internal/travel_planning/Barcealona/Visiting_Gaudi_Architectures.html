Visiting Antonio Gaudi architectures and moving around Barcealona city.

If you are in Barcealona , then Gaudi architectures are a must visit.The city contains a well equipped commutation facilities supported by buses and metros.

La Sagrada Familia Basilica

It is one of the most famous of Antonio Gaudi and one of the largest sky scrappers in the city.It is a giant Basilica that is under construction since 1882.

La Sagrada Familia Opening Hours

  1. October to March 9:00 to 18:00
  2. April to September: 9:00 to 20:00
  3. December 25th and 26th, January 1st and 6th: 9:00 to 14:00

Getting there

  1. Metro: Lines 2 and 5
  2. Station name : Sagrada Familia Station

Audio guide Languages

  1. Catalan,Spanish,English,French,German,Italian,Portuguese,Russian,Chinese,Japanese.

Guided tour with a guide

  1. Languages :Catalan, Spanish, English, French, Italian, German.

Ticket Price

  1. 29 Euros : Entrance ticket,audio guide and climb to one of the towers(Tower on Passion facade or Nativity Facade)
  2. 24 Euros : Entrance ticket,audio guide and ticket to Gaudi museum in Park Guell.
  3. 24 Euros : Entrance ticket along with a guided visit.
  4. 22 Euros : Entrance ticket and audio guide included in the price.
  5. 15 Euros : Comes only with entrance ticket.

How to book?

You can purchase tickets using their website by choosing the ticket of your choice ,date and timing of your visit.

  1. Official website to purchase tickets.

What you need to know ?

  1. Queue size is huge on any given day and it is mandatory to pre-book tickets if you are to save your time.
  2. Online tickets come with pre-defined slot timings and you are not allowed before or after the timing.
  3. Only one of the towers are included in the price and you cannot visit both.
  4. You need two to three hours to spend at this place.


Park Guell

A historic stylish park constructed on the orders of Eusebi Guell.You need half a day for travelling,visiting the park and coming back.

What does that park have in offering?

  1. House of Gaudi where he spent significant part of his life.
  2. Gaudí dragon fountain that is at the entrance to Guell park
  3. Pathway supported by twisting rock pillars
  4. A huge Mosaic seating area with multi coloured tiles.

Park Guell Opening Hours

  1. March 27 to May 1 : 08.00 to 20.30 (last entry time at 19.30)
  2. May 2 to August 28: 08.00 to 21.30 (last entry time at 20.30)
  3. October 30 to March 26 08.30 to 18.15 (last entry time at 17.30)
  4. August 29 to October 29: 08.00 to 20.30 (last entry time at 19.30)

Getting there by Metro

Please be prepared for a 20 min walk by foot if you are travelling by metro.

  1. Metro: Lines 3
  2. Station name : Vallcarca or Lesseps station

Getting there by Bus

Bus stops just opposite to the park's main entrance and the name of the stop is Carretera Carmel-Park Guell

  1. Line 24 : From Plaça Catalunya headting towards Passeig de Gracia.Bus stop is Carretera Carmel-Park Guell
  2. Line 92 : From Trelawny-Passeig Marítim headting towards Sagrada Familia and Hospital de Sant Pau.Bus stop is Carretera Carmel-Park Guell.
