Things you should know when you are in Barcealona

  1. People speak a different language called Catalan and not Spanish.
  2. If you are for more than a day then try purchasing multi use tickets ( T10 or anyother suggested above.)
  3. Look out for Barcealona football matches on the day you visit Barcealona.They are a treat to watch.
  4. You should try Catalan cuisines - Paella(variety of sea food),Sagria ( famous wine made of fruits) and Tapas ( a variety of appetizer or snack).
  5. Visit to famous Gaudi architectures.Pride and treasure of Barcealona city.
  6. Be cautious while travelling around La Rambles.Though it is projected as pick pocketting spot , all you need to do is be careful and sensitive.I spent close to 4 hours around La Rambles roaming,having my dinner and it was completely safe.
