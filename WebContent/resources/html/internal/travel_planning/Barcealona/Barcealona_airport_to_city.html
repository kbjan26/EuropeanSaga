What is a T10 ticket ?

T10 is kind of a season ticket that allows you to use the metro ride and bus ride 10 times on the whole.Each time you commute you need to swipe it using the travel card and it expires at the count of 10.Zone-1 travel card covers all the major city center.

How much is the price of zone-1 T10 travel card?

The price is 9.95 euros.

Can I use this card's ride to travel in Renfe R2 to head to the city?

Yes , you can absolutely use and save your money from buying a separate Renfe train ticket.Also by this you do spend 6 euros one way by using Aerobus service.
