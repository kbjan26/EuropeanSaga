Scuba diving at L'estartit costa brava.

Scuba diving was on top of my checklist due to the Indian movie Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara and for the fact that I recently accomplished learning swimming.

I did it at a place called Zindagi L'estartit, a costal place of Costa Brava and involves a day trip from Barcealona. The school provides you with basic gear set and training before you jump into the water.

What does the day trip involve from Barcealona?

  1. Round trip in train from Barcelona Sants to Girona.It takes 30 mins and approximately 28 Euros.
  2. You can book the tickets through go euro website
  3. www.goeuro.com
  4. Bus travel from Girona to l'estartit by AMPSA bus service provider.It takes exactly one hour.
  5. Scuba diving will cost you anywhere between 65 to 70 Euros including photos depending on the school.
  6. Prior appointment with the driving schools.If your arrival is not confirmed then they will not reserver your slot.
  7. Carry a towel along with you to dry yourself once you are done.
  8. Some crazy mind with high adrenaline rush.

What you should know?

  1. Book appropriate train depending on your reservation.
  2. Bus timings of AMPSA service provider are only once every hour.
  3. Weekend timings are scarce and it is better to reserve it for weekdays if you are travelling from Barcealona.
