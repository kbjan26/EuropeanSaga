Iceland Planner - 6 days in Iceland.

How to do day trips from Reykjavik ?

Fortunately, I had 6 days to spend in Iceland and all my trips were guided trips.There are several agencies who does group trips,guided trips,private guide tours.I chose www.grayline.is/ since their pricing scale fitted my budget. My base was in the captial,Reykjavik.All the spots are very far away from Reykjavik and it almost takes 12 to 14 hours to cover and come back.The public transportation is almost none to support in this cause unless or until you are planning for a road trip.

Where to stay in the Reykjavik ?

Northern Lights ?.

This natural phenomena is a traveller's dream and I must say I have been fortuntate enough to witness this marvel.

If you are in Iceland please ensure you include this in your first day of your Itinerary.

The chances are high that if you are unfortunate that you will never see Northern Lights at all.

If you are doing with guide agency like grayline , the ticket is valid for one year and they take you continously until they have succeeded.

The weather out there is extremely cold and you cannot think of playing around with your camera to capture photos.

If you are crazy on photos check out various tutorials online as to how to use your camera for capturing the lady aurora.

Carry a tripod to capture it using your camera.
