Blue Lagoon , Iceland.

Important things that you must know about Blue Lagoon.

The first and foremost thing is that it is no where near to the city and it is far away.

It is close to 45 mins to Keflavik airport.

If your flight arrives in Keflavik during the day time then head straight to Blue Lagoon before you head into the city.If you do not have a car then you can book any of the guide tours that pre-books your slot and has pick from the airport and also dropping you at your destination.Enquire with any of the service providers online.

Never plan for a day trip or half day trip from the city wasting your time.Your itinerary can be spared for destinations due to the travel time to this place from the Capital.

It is a man made extravaganza present enroute between Keflavik airport to Reykjavik.The heat is primarily uses the volcanic landscape near by to keep the water warm all the time.It is basically a geo thermal plant.

No more than three hours is required at this place including the steam bath,spa and relaxing around the pool.

Ensure you carry a waterproof cover for your camera or phone if you are keen on taking photos insider the water.

Carry your own towel in order to avoid the cost of 5 euros to be paid for renting a towel.
