La Tomatina- BUNOL - Spain.

La Tomatina - the most cherished,most celebrated,most awaited of all my solo saga.Probably the only event I was waiting for so much time and planned several months prior and the one I did not want to miss.

Tomatina is a traditional world famous festival where people gather from all around the globe to meet to have the experience and fun of throwing tomatoes at each other.

What you need to know about this festival ?

Rules and Regulations

  1. Hard objects are strictly not allowed.
  2. Selfie sticks are not allowed.
  3. Always stay away from the trucks.
  4. Abide by the volunteer's guidelines.
  5. Squash the tomatoes before you hit others.

When and Where it is celebrated ?

It is celebrated on the last Wednesday of August every year in the town of Bunol near Valencia Spain. The event happens between 11AM and 12PM leaving spell bounded and amazed in the end.

It is held every year in the town of Bunol near Valencia , Spain

What happens on the day of the event?

People from all across the globe start gathering around 8 AM in the town of Bunol and start gearing up.Several companies organise day trips from all across cities in Spain.If you are arriving by a guided tour you will be provided with a t-shirt and entry ticket to the festival.

Days Trips are organised from various parts of Spain

It is possible to participate in this event by doing a day trip from places like Valencia,Sevilla,Barcealond,Madrid.The cost varies from agency to agency and also depends on the packages that you choose.

Carry along water proof shoes.

Once the truck start rolling in you will see the entire place flooded with tomatoes.It is better to wear a water proof shoe rather than wasting a sports shoe or wearing a slipper.

Carry water proof cases and shoulder bag.

In case if you wanter capture the event and record it for ever then you must carry a water proof case for your camera and phone.However , carrying it and taking photos is at your own risk due to the heavy crowd you will never know what may happen.Also carry a water proof should bag to give further protection to your belongings.

Wear swimming suits and goggles.

Wear jersey kind of attire that does not absorb water in case if you are travelling on your own without a guided trip.Also wear goggles to protect your eyes.
