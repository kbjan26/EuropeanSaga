Schafberg Cogwheel Railway ,St.Wolfgangsee,St.Gilgen.

Travelling in the cogwheel train at Schafberg Mountains is a MUST thing to do when you are in Salzkammergut region.

How to get there from St Gilgen ?

Get down at St.Giglen main bus station if you are travelling by 150 from Salzburg towards bad Ischl.Get to the ferry point that is few mins walk from the cable car point.The lane getting down beside the cable car station in St.Giglen takes you to the ferry boarding point.

What to do in St.Giglen?

St.Gilgen is the heart of of Salzgermmut lake districts and is the transit point for other lake districts. There are many things to do in St.Gilgen itself including the breathtaking cable car ride.

  1. Have a walk around the lake and its beautiful
  2. Try out the water sports that are in the offering.
  3. Take the cable car ride that goes to the top and it costs you 24 Euros.
  4. Do the panaromic hike around the St.Giglen top.
