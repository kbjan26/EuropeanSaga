HallStatt-Dachstein Planning.

Where is HallStatt located ?

Hall is a tiny little wonder on earth located in the Salzkammergut region.

How to get there ?

You can reach HallStatt from both Vienna and Salzburg.It takes 5 hours by from Vienna and 2 hours from Salzburg.

From Vienna.

  1. It takes almost 5 hours including your waiting time
  2. Route A: Vienna-West-Train-Station (Wien-Westbahnhof)- Attnang Puchheim-Hallstatt Station-Hallstatt Markt
  3. Route B : Vienna Main-Train-Station (Wien-Hauptbahnhof)- Leoben-Stainach Irdning-Hallstatt Station-Hallstatt Markt
  4. Use OBB train website for all your bookings and time table information.

From Salzburg.

  1. You can reach both by Bus(150 & 542) and Train.
  2. Salzburg-Bad Ischl (Bus 150) , Bad Ischl-Hallstatt Gosaumuhle (Bus 542)
  3. Be prepared to buy ticket by cash if you are travelling by Bus.
  4. 150 bus time table
  5. 542 bus time table
  6. 543 bus time table
  7. Train route : Salzburg-Attnang-Puchheim-Hallstatt Bahnhof
  8. If you are travelling by train ,then you will have to take a small ferry to get to the Hallstatt town.It costs 2.60 Euros and gives you the best majestic view of the town.
  9. Use OBB website for all your bookings and time table information.OBB train website
  10. Bus Website

Things to do in HallStatt?

When you are in HallStatt you need more than a day if you want to cover salt mines,Dachstein glacier and viewing platform.

How to get to Dachstein glacier and Viewing platform ?

If you reach HallStatt town by train then you need to go to HallStatt Lahn stop in the town center.From there bus 543 takes you to Obertraun.From there you need to take a cable car to reach to the glacier spot and viewing platform.

Things to know for glacier visit and Viewing platform?

  1. You need 4 to 5 hours to cover both the Dachstein glacier and Viewing platform.
  2. Dachstein glacier visit is a guided tour and is bi-lingual( Germand and English)
  3. The temperature inside the glacier is almost 0 degree.
  4. Carry warm jackets to protect yourself.
  5. The whole package cost is 41.10 for adults.
  6. First dropping point is Glacier and another cable car takes you further up to the viewing platform.

Useful Tips

  1. You can place your luggage at the tourist information center until 5 PM for 5 Euros.
  2. Avoid taking ferry tour in Hallstat if you are travelling by train since you will be taking a small boat to reach the other side of the town.
  3. Try to spend one and half days if you want to cover everything.
