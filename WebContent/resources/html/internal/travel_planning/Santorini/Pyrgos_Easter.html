Why is Easter in Pyrgos so special in Pyrgos?

In Pyrgos , Greece , easter is celebrated the best way on Easter Friday.The entire village is decorated by placing little aluminium cans all across. At night between 8.30 - 9 AM after the mass prayers at the church the aluminium cans containing flammable materials are lighted by fire and the entire village glows into a magnificient wonder and its a unique phenomea of its own tradition.The view of the town is so spectacular that it has turned to be a famous tourist spot with visitor from all across the globe.

How is the festival celebrated and how long it lasts?

It is celebrated during Greek Easter Friday.Everyone gathers on Easter Friday for the procession of Epitaph.Epitaph is decorared with flowers and they start chanting religious prayers.As and when the gathering start for the procession , the villagers standing atop of every house light the aluminum cans with flammable material.The momentum slowly spreads across the village and the entire landscape glows with candle light experience.The celebration lasts till 11 PM before the crowd slowly start dispersing.
