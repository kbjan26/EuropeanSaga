Reaching and Getting Around Santorini.

Santorini is a wonderful tiny small island.It is a heavenly paradise on earth and can be reached in many ways.

How to reach Santorini from Athens?

From Athens,Santorini can be reached either by ferry,speed boat or an airplane.The fastest way to reach Santorini is obviously by flight which can cost you between 30 to 80 euros depending on how early you book.If you are travelling by ferry then be prepared to 4 to 5 hours including your travel from Athens airport to Pireaus port from where you can catch either a ferry or a speed boat.

By flight

If you are travelling from parts of Europe then you can check for direct flights to Santorini if not then Athens will be your most likely transit.Major air carrier have flights to Santorini if you flying from Athens.

  1. KAYAK airlines
  2. OLYMPIC airlines(domestic carrier)
  3. RYANAIR

By ferry from Pireaus

If you are travelling from Athens to Santorinin by ferry then Pireaus port is the place from where you catch ferry or speed boats run by various carriers.Blue star carriers or Hellenic seaways.

How to reach Pireaus port from Athens airport ?

By Bus

Bus station to Pireaus port is located just outside the airport between Exit 4 and 5 at the arrivals part.Bus X 96 takes appromiately an hour if the traffic is huge.Buses run throughout the day starting at 00.05 AM in the morning till 23.35 PM in the night.

  1. X96 timetable
  2. 00.05 to 6 : Every 30 mins
  3. 6 to 22.35 : Every 20 mins
  4. 22.35 to 23.35 : 22.35,23.05,23.35
  5. Ticket price : 6 euros one way.
  6. X96 Ticket price information

Ferry from Pireaus port to Thira Port Santorini

There are two main carriers who ply between two ports every day and make sure of the timings of the ferries which are subjective for frequent changes. You can either buy tickets online ( recommended) or directly form the ticketing offices located in the port.

  1. Blue Star Ferrywebsite
  2. Hellenic Seawayswebsite

It is always better to book speed boats rather than slow ferris due to the time taken.Now that we have understood ways to reach Athens to Santorini the next part is to know how to roam around Santorini.

How to travel from Fira Port to town ?

Don pre book taxis in advance or hire a taxi after reaching port.There is a local bus which waits keeping in mind the timing of the arrival of boats and ferries.it costs only 2.30 euros to head to fira.Finding the bus station is no big deal can be easily spotted.

  1. By Taxi : Approximately 16 Euros
  2. By Bus(cheaper and recommended) : 2.30 Euros

How to roam around Santorini ?

You can travel around Santorini by Bus,Taxi and Bike.However , the best way to cover Santorini is by hiring and renting a Bike.

By Bus

If you are depending on the commutation by Bus then it is better to know of the timings.Cost of one way travel is between 1.80 and 2.50 Euros.The only inconvenience here is you are bound the schedule and timings of the buses.

  1. Fira Santorini Bus Timetable

By Bike

If you are thinking to make the most of your time when you are in Santorini and if you intend to cover the places that are not covered by Bus commutation , then hiring Bike is the best option.For Europeans who have driving license it is granted at ease and if you are a non European you could make use of the influence of your hosts to rent a bike.There are many renting shops in Fira.

  1. Rent per day : 15 Euros on an average.
  2. One day Petrol cost : 5 Euros.
