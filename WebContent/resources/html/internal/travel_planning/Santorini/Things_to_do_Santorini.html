Santorini - 4 days in this wonderful landscape.

With 4 days or more you are bound to have a heavenly expedition in this wonderful island covering both crowded and secluded places.There is no better way to roam around than hiring a bike.When I landed there, I quickly realised I should own a bike.Also parts of the island are best covered only with private transport and not with the public.Renting a bike is very cheap and 5 euros a day is all required to fill in your tank and roam around.

How to travel from Fira Port to town ?

Don pre book taxis in advance or hire a taxi after reaching port.There is a local bus which waits keeping in mind the timing of the arrival of boats and ferries.it costs only 2.30 euros to head to fira.Finding the bus station is no big deal can be easily spotted.

  1. By Taxi : Approximately 16 Euros
  2. By Bus(cheaper and recommended) : 2.30 Euros

Must do things when you are in Santorini.

Walk around Oia

Earthly heaven is what I would call this place.Every turn you take is majestic and the views are simply stunning.Go crazy and walk two to three hours in and around this town.



Sunet at Oia.

One of the most soughted after happenings when you are in Santorini is sunset.There are many viewing points but the best view can be obtained from the historic castle spot.Ensure you are there 2 to 3 hours before to get settled and get the full view of it.



Visit to Amoudi Bay - food and cliff jumping.

Amoudi Bay is one of the sexiest spots in Oia town and you can reach it when you further head down after reaching Oia.

Food is something that is not to be missed and I am told that you get very good star fish and smoked lamb.Being a vegan , I had not been fortunate enough.Food is quite expensive though.Also you can try is the famous donkey ride.



If you walk further to Amoudi Bay you have a cliff that is quite enough to jump from.Plan yourself do it to an experience of lifetime.


Light house cliffs

Spot that can be covered only if you have private vehicle.Cannot be reached using public transport.The views that you get will leave you awe struck.

Red Beach and Black Sand Beach

Another hot spots not to be missed when you are in Santorini.When I was there , black sand beach was a big rough and could not swim where as red beach was as cool as cucumber.Spent almost 3 hours in the latter.


Fira - Oia hike

A refreshing 10 KM hike from Fira town to Oia.The route is pretty well signed and easily conquered in less than three hours.A must do activity to see route from Fira-Oia.
