Hostel life experience in Europe.

Ever since I started travelling alone , Hostel has been my primary choice.What started out of compulsion in order to save money became my desire on the long run.

Hostel culture in Europe is something that I am inspired and cherish too.There cannot be a professional,secured place with greatest of ambience.The acquiantance you make with strangers and the stories that you hear and share with them are fun,insane and inspiring.It is an experience that is a must every traveller who is on a budget travel.

What are my preferences ?

Most of the selection are common and the only key thing that I look out for is kitchenette facilities.The reason being a vegeterian and Indian foodie who cannot starve without some spicy food.I always ensure once in a day I have some self cooked Indian meal.

Some of the best remembrances from the wonderful journey are below.

What makes hostel 360 so special to me ?

World class staff who are highly knowledged about Barcealona city.I have not seen anywhere where the staff where equipped with details so much.

Catalunya cuisine were served for free every night - it was either Tapas,Sagira or Paella that was in the offering.

Free walking tour of La Rambles and Barcealona city.It was also very near to Plac catalunya.



What makes it best of all?

First impression is always the best one and KEX did not disappoint me.

The hostel is so huge spanning to couple of floors and it is maintained in the best of way though the influx of visitors are more.

An in-house spacious bar to keep members entertained till 2 AM in the morning.

It was one of those trips to Strokkur Geysir and to my dismay had a fall and twisted my ankle so much that I was hopping the entire day.Strain to the hurted part became worse during the night and it started swelling making time so miserable.

Resources are at scare in Iceland with very less population and I could not go anywhere for first aid.Staff at KEX hostel were kind enough to treat me with huge Ice pack to reduce the swelling in order to make my stay and upcoming visits comfortable.
