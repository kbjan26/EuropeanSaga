A splendid beautiful place along Amalfi coast is Postiano.Sorrento,Positano,Amalfi Coast are three beautiful places near by to each other.

How to reach Positano from Naples ?

If you are travelling from Naples then you will have to take a direct train to Sorrento which is almost an hour and from there Positano is one hour by bus and Amalfi coast comes next.Since I was having Naples as my base chose Positano as my destination for a day trip.The overall journey from Naples to Sorrento is almost 2.30 hours including your waiting time.

The round trip train cost is 5.50 Euros for Sorrento. From Sorrento the bus to Positano is 2.20 Euros.

Positano is a little coastal town with houses on the hill. It's such a scenic place to relax and enjoy your day at ease. You get down at fermata bus stop and from then small walk down the hill takes you to the busy beach surrounded by restaurants.The tourist information team sits at the bus stop and they provide you all the information you want.Also please be aware that the return ticket has to be purchased from Tabacci shop and not in the bus.The return buses from Positano are heavily crowded so be prepared for a standing journey irrespective of whether you are heading to Alamfi coast or Sorrento.

Two famous things in Positano are the chocolates of Lemon flavour and perfumes made of Lemon Oil.
