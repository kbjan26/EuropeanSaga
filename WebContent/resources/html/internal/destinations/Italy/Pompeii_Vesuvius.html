Naples - Pompeii day trip.

How to reach Pompeii from Naples ?

Pompeii is just 30 mins from Naples and the train that goes to Sorrento stops at Pompeii Scavi.Go to Circumvesuviana station and take a direct train to Sorrento and get down at Pompeii scavi.The round trip train cost from Naples is 5.10 Euros.

Right opposite to the train station location is the Pompeii Scavi.It is one of the well preserved excavation spots I believe and also the space that it covers is huge.The entrance fee to the site is 13 Euros and the audio guide is 5 euros separately.

When you exit the station there is a tourist agency who provide guided tour for both Mount Vesuvis and ruins of Pompeii.I chose to do guided trip for Vesuvius since I was travelling alone.The roundtrip cost is 22 euros.Mount Vesuvis is supposed to be one of the most active volcanic spots in Europe and the eruption lead to the destruction of Pompeii in the past.You can still see some Sulphur gas coming out due to high temperature in the region.

There are two bus that you take to reach Vesuvis. The first one travels along the hill and the next one travels along the most rugged path and can be too annoying.After you reach the spot , you will have to hike for 20 mins before you the actual mountain.You are given close to hour and half to hike around the place which gives some splendid views of Pompei and Amalfi Coast.

It was evening when I scaled down and headed to Pompeii immediately.You need atleast three to four hours to see the ruins and excavation findings.
