St Gilgen.

Both Mondsee and St.Wolfgangsee are beautiful lake districts in Salzkammgerut region

Where ever you are , you need to come to St.Gilgen to go to Mondsee and Wolfgangsee. The former can be reached by bus easily and for the latter it is better to take a direct ferry from St.Gilgen.Watch out for the ferry timings when you are in Wolfgangsee.It takes half an hour by boat from St.Gilgen. You can see beautiful green spots in the water due to the presence of limestones underneath the surface of the water.

Mondsee is a very tiny lake district with ancient church,park and a lake where you can take boat ride.The boat ride gives you a panaromic view of the lake.

The main activity in the Wolfgangsee region is the Cogwheel ride up to the top which runs by Coal.It takes you back to the ancient days the railways operated and the again the view enroute are simply spectacular.The cost of the ride is 41.10 and the journey to the top takes approximately 30 minutes.You have a restaurant at the top to refresh and reenergize.After reaching the top you can do hiking in several directions climbing down and up again.Ensure you reach the place pretty early to experience all this.

When travelling by train you can get down at schefelbug stop right opposite to which is the cog wheel ride station.
