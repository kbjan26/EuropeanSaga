The main lake district and another beautiful one in the Salzgermmurt region is St.Gilgen.This is the connecting and transit points for other districts in this region

My last day in this region was spent in St.Gilgen.The most famous activites here in this region are the cable car ride and the hiking at the top of the mountain which takes close to two hours and gives you excellent aerial views.The cable car ticket cost 24 euros.

The cable car ride to the top is quite slow,nice and gives you enough time to view the path scaled up.

The start and finish of the hiking point is the place where you get down from the cable car. It is kind of panaromic hike where you end up as to where you started and hence you get 360 degree view of the lake districts.You also see many experience para gliders gearing for the activity preparing themselves for the ride.

After scaling down , the lake in St.Gilgen had quite lot of water activities to experience and chose one of the water rides before winding off and saying adios.
