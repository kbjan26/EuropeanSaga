Day In Vienna.

Vienna , a city plethora of churches,museums,palace,historical erections and probably the best city I have ever been which has phenomenal architectures.

With only a day in stake , I tried to visit some of the best spots though I am big appreciator of arts and architectures.

After tiring train journey , I landed in Vienna around 7 AM in the morning and head straight to Wombats city hostel lounge where I had booked my accomodation.As soon as you land in Vienna it is better to to buy 24 hour pass if you are going to stay for only one day and it covers unlimited round trips using buses ,metros and trams.

After quick refresh , headed to the old town.My first stop was at St. Stephen's Cathedral.The ancient giant Church stands tall and one of the main attractions in the old town.After a brief pit stop, commuted around the city by foot covering State Opera House,Austrian National Library ,Parliament Building.

Second half of the day was spent completely in Schonbrunn Palace,another historical and important monument in the city.The stretch of the gardens and the area this place covers is humoungous.
