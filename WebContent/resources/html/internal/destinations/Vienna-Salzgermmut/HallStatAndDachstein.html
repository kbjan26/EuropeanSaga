Hall Stat - Dachstein glacier - Viewing point.

Be prepared for a busy schedule if you are to cover both Hall stat and Dachstein glacier and further scaling up to see the five finger spot.I had booked a train from Vienna to Hall stat which had transit at Attnang-Puchheim Bahnhof. I had pre booked my tickets through OBB website

OBB website

The journey from the HallStatt station to the town is through the ferry service. One way fare costs 2.60 Euros and is one of the rides that I have ever taken. It gives you a stunning and fantabulous view of the entire from the other end.The town is so small and an overnight stay here no doubt would have been so pleasant which I missed it unfortunately due to lack of planning. I had my accomodation in Lake fuschl area.

After roaming around for a while headed to the bus station which takes the tourists to Obertraun from where you get the cable car to scale up to Dachstein glacier spot and five finger point.Please be aware of the bus timings since you have only one bus every hour.Also near the bus station is the tourist office where you can gather all required information about the place and park your luggage for few Euros if need be which I did since I was carrying a huge baggage.

Both the glacier and the five fingers can be covered only if you are at the place very early.The last bus back from Obertraun to HallStat is around 16.40 PM and hence you must in a position to have covered both the places before that.

Both Dachstein glacier and five finger point has to be covered by taking cable car.Whole package costs you 41.10 Euros for adults.Dachstein glacier is the first stop using cable car and further scaling up takes you the point where you can reach to five finger point by 20 minute walk.

Visit to the Dachstein cave is a guided tour where the guide briefs the crowd in both German and English.The entire walk inside the ice cave lasts for almost 30 mins and the temperature inside is freezing cold.You are taken through the entire stretch and brief the history ,science behind the formation of the glacier.Ensure you carry a coat since it will be freezing cold inside.After completing the glacier walk headed back to the spot to scale further up using the cable to the viewing point.

The aerial view from here gives you a beautiful view of both HallStatt and Obertraun.You will also be able to see people doing para gliding down to the town.Views are again simply stunning.
