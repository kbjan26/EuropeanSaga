Visit to Kinderjik.

Historic Windmills is famous both in Amsterdam and Rotterdam.If you are in Amsterdam , then Zaanse Schans is the place to visit and if you are covering the stretch of Rotterdam , then you can visit Kinderjik.

It is located quite far from Rotterdam central and takes an hour to reach to this place by bus.There is a direct bus number 90 which takes you to Kinderjik.You need minimum of 3 hours at this place walking around the lake to see the historic giant erections.

Several giant historic windmills made of woods resides around a small little lake.Scattered across the landscape , it takes you two to three hours to walk around and see everything.
