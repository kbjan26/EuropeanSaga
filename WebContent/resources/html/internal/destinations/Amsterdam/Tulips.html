Tulips - earthly beauty.

The only reason why I have visited Amsterdam twice is for this place.An earthly heaven I would call it.The journey started with entire day allocated.Located at 45 mins journey from Amsterdam central, Keukenof gardens,has both mad made and natural fields at its stake.

Flowers here blossom between March end and lasts till mid of May every year.Both man made gardens and natural gardens are a huge crowd puller.Witnessing huge variety of tulips and cycling around the large natural gardens will leave you captivated.

Ensure you allocate whole to be spent at kekuneof.Start your day early and ensure you are at the boarding point (in my case it was from Schipol airport.).There are two areas to view the abundance of the flowers - the man made gardens and the natural flower fields.Try to book tickets online which includes the transportation costs along with the entrance tickets.

The man made gardens covers a huge area and take around 3 hours.There are several varieties of Tulips planted and they are charm to witness.There are places to relax,energize,to dine inside the gardens.


Once you are done with the gardens comes the next big thing , the natural flower fields.The fields have shades of flowes grown by the farmers and they blossom between March last week and lasts till mid of May which pulls crowd from all over the world.

The natural flower fields are spread across several KMs.Near the entrance of the gardens , you can hire a cycle which will cost you around 12 euros to go the natural flower fields.There will be designated and signed paths to follow in order to reach there and the locals will be able to give you a better picture of the presence of flowers in each of those places.You have several shops enroute to stop by.
