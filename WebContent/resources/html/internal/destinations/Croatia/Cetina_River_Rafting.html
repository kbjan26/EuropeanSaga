Cetina River rafting , Omis, Split.

Landed around 9.30 AM in the morning of August10th in split. Had planned for cetina rafting afternoon at 13.30 and was heading on a tight schedule. To my dismay at the luggage belt counter was around 45mins making it more impatient. I then headed out to see buses for split and was able to catch the one about to leave. I had to stand through out. The climate was hot and the traffic on the roads was pathetic. Split does not have metro facilities unlike other cities in Europe. It was feel at home experience since I am from India. Finally reached split around 11.15 and headed to hostel by taxi. I didn't want to risk my time finding the route myself. I was charged 60 kunas for just fee mins of drive.

I did a checkin and completed my brunch that I had prepared. Had to come to the bus station again to catch bus to omis. The bus was 60 and had started from thee station waiting neat the signal. I ran all the way to request the driver to open the door and he kindly obliged. Again the journey was hell taking one hour and fifteen mins. Finally reached around 13.10 and sharp at 1.30 Mate came to pick us up. We were picked up at studenac shopping centre. We went all the way up in car to the starting point. We were told the entire journey is 12kms and it would take three hours.It indeed took almost 3 hours to complete including pit stops taken.

We were dressed up and started around 14.30. Guide name was karlos, a young energetic enthusiastic guy. I teamed up with a family from hungray and we were 7on the whole. Enroute was pretty interesting covering rough paths. Midway we got down at a point to go through a small cave where the temperature was freezing. Though the time spent inside was few mins the experience was amazing with fresh drinking water coming from the branches of the trees. After covering certain distance we parked at a small cliff where everyone was allowed to jump. There was dedicated guide who was travelling in a separate boat to capture the entire journey of us and we were charged 230 kunas plus 100 for the photos.

Croatia Experience

I contacted the guy Mate the organizer through facebook just a day before and the overall price was just 230 Kuna.

You cannot go barefoot.Ensure you wear water proof sandals.

Photos are recorded in a CD and charge is 100 Kunas.
