PlitVice and KRKA National Parks.

Croatia is full of landscapes and two of the key places is PlitVice and KRKA national parks.Both natural reserves has the best water bodies that you will ever see.The former is 4 hours away and the latter is 2 hours away from Split.

There are plenty of buses available if you want to do it as a day trip.You can either book it online through bus croatia website or purchase at the ticket counter.PlitVice one day ticket is 180 Kuna or two day ticket is 280 PLN.Ticket price for KRKA is 280 overall (130 Kuna for the first waterfall and if you want to scale further up visiting two island at the top it costs you 150 Kuna by boat.)

PlitVice is a huge landscape spanning several KMs.It has to be covered through designated signed path.There are four routes A,B,C,D that you choose from with D being the longest. You can wander between through boat and buses.Buses can be used any number of times and ferry journey gives you some of the best views of the lake.

There are three ferry points named P1,P2,P3 and three bus points named ST1,ST2,ST3. Ferry trip between P3 and P1 is allowed only once it takes almost 15 mins.

Started to PlitVice by taking early morning bus at 7 from Split and reached at 10.30 A.M.It was drizzling and cloudy when I landed.PlitVice lakes consists of close to 100 tiny waterfalls dropping out from tiny cliffs across the stretch of the landscape.They are treat to watch.If you take any of the routes you will see waterfalls of different sizes through out giving stunning views.The place is unharmed not invaded and clean. The climate was cloudy which made our walk easy.Ensure you carry food and water along to help you to re-energize yourself.

Krka was allocated was for day 4 and prior bookings were done using getbybus.com. Departure was at 7 and return was around 19.30.Another hectic day ahead was I thought.on the contrary krka is one of the best places for relaxation. From the entrance you are taken by the boat to the national park and it's close to 30 mins journey. The large waterfall is beautiful and you are allowed to take a dip there.

The entries path around it is 2Km and you can complete in less than an hour since I completed it early I decided to scale up further to visit two islands by boat. The overall journey is 4 hours including the transit but it gives you a highly relaxing sensation. I had my kindle and was just reading taking enough rest. The total price was 280 kuna.

The entire day was so peaceful apart from two unforgettable incidents I had lost my entrance ticket before entering the park. (this is something that I am always good at). I told them I lost it during boat journey. They were kind enough to allow me inside believing I bought a ticket on my return I decided to depart early around 6.30 and made it on time for the bus. But before I could grab a seat I realised I had forgotten the camera.

It was shit feeling and had to board off. It was quite a few minutes on my way back to the park entrance thinking where I lost finally I arrived in the reception and told them I had lost my camera. Fortunately someone had given it and they were holding it. They told it was found in the park. The incident left me an unforgettable mark on this place
