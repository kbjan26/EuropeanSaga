Solheimajokull glacier hike.

The hike activity was one of the most awaited events in my entire itinerary.This glacier is one of the largest in Iceland and is called Home of Sun ( which I remember by heart because the guide questioned me several times and ensure its remembered for ever).Again , the travel to this route is via Skogafoss waterfall and is very far away from the captial.I reserved the slot online through gray line web site.I had also rented hiking boots,rain coat suiting my size.

Our guide was a danish and was an experienced lad.We were split into groups of ten.We were briefed about the do and dont's before even we could start on the history of the place.They take minute care of every single tourist since a slip could cause severe damages.

The hike itself lasted almost two hours with pit stops at several spots where the guide described of its significance.Finaly we reached the top where we could drink pure water melted from Ice.At the top , we could also see a measurement device to check the height of the ice and the receding measurement.It was alarming to know that there is a significant descent in the measurement due to climatic changes.The melting ice flows into the ocean is what we were told.
