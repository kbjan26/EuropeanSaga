Golden Circle - Strokkur geysir.

After having extraordinary day one in Iceland , the second day was allocated for golden circle waterfalls and Strokkur geysir.

Its located almost 300 kms from Reykjavik.I was picked up around 8.45 AM from hostel.All guided tours includes hostel pick up and drop.We headed for the spot at 9.30 AM from the tourist office.The cost of the trip was close to 85 euros.

Our first stop was at golden circle waterfalls.It was the start of the spring and end of winter and hence had an amazing view of the water flowing down through the ice.The guide who accompanied us briefed us about the waterfalls and the ancient parliament located near to it.The landscapes enroute to this place was the best of all I captured during my entire stay.It was a sheer scenic beauty to witness.

After a long walk around the falls , we had additional time to have our lunch.Iceland was my first solo travelling destination and had very little time to plan.Bought tomato soup and a burger in the hotel and it costed me 30 euros for single meal.Iceland is one of the costly destinations that I have ever been.Thats where I decided to start cooking myself from next day to save some money.

The next journey was headed to Strokkur geyser.We were breifed about high temperatures and water eruptions in that area due to high volanic activities in the past.I did not believe until I saw what descibed was true.The water here erupts almost 30 meters for every 5 to 6 mins throughout the year.I captured both the video and pictures of this unique natual happening.The day was over then after which we returned back to the captial.One long tiring day was over and never realised all my day trips from the city would be the same.
