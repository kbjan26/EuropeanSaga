Iceland is one of the costliest European destinations that I have ever been.My first day lunch was 30 euros which was an eye opener to start cooking during the trips.If possible carry whatever you can before you land there.I carried some snacks which lasted for two days.Below are the points which any budget traveller might find highly useful.

If you like hostel culture ,try KEX hostel which has excellent facilities in store.

Take a container along with you to pack foods for your travel.

BONUS is the cheapest super market located in the city.However, please be aware of the wierd opening timings it has.

Grayline tour operator is the cheapest I could find of all operating agenices.

WOW air the cheap airliner that commutes from various destinations.

If you are in groups, then you may hire a car avoiding costly day trips.
