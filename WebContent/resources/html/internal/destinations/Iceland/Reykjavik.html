Day around Reykjavik.

Reykjavik , capital of Iceland is a tiny little place and there are some beautiful spots to cover.Main attractions are Cathedral,Harbor,Museum and tiny art galleries.

The stretch around the harbour lake is flcoked by tourists and early morning joggers.This was located right opposite to my hostel and I started my day with walk along the harbor lake.It gives you some stunning views.Located near by the harbor is the Reykjavik art gallery.The admission ticket was around 1500 ISK and it includes free entry to other 2 art galleries located in the city.If you are an art lover you would not be disappointed with the things available for display.After sending quite some time here ,I headed for the cathedral which is the most popular attraction in the city.

Make sure of the timings of the cathedral on the day of your visit.Cathedral is the main attraction the city giving beautiful aerial view of the city.Visit to the top costs you 900 Iceland Kronor.

Take walk around the main street to see what it has in offereing.It has lots and lots of restro bars selling drinks at high price.For Asians, there is Nepalese,Thai and Pakistani restaurant.Be aware of the prices of the products in the high street here.They are usually higher that what you expect.In case if you are looking for a decent and cheap super market ,then there is one called BONUS.
