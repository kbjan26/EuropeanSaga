Skogafoss Waterfall.

This waterfall is located along the south coast route and is one of the heart throbs if you are travelling along south coast.

I had pitstop at this place on two occassions.One was on the day we headed for Jokusarlon and the other while visiting Solheimajokull glacier.Two occassions gave me the experience of two different weather conditions.

The cliffs from which the water drops is simply stunning and the captures at this place looked so graphical.One day was completely cloudy and the other day we had sun shining with the back drop of rainbows in place.The foot path along side of the water fall take you to the top giving you an aerial pictue of this scenic beauty.
