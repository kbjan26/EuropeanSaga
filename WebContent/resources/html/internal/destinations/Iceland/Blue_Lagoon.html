Blue Lagoon.

Landed in Keflavik around 14.10 PM and headed to this place right away since its enroute to Rejyavik.I had booked all my trips with a tour company called gray lined.I had scheduled this visit for 15.30 prior to my landing and the bus was ready waiting outside.I was the only passenger in the bus and yet they did take me to the spot with any hassle.

This place is artifically constructed with high land scape surroundings and one of the busiest with each and every tourist visiting before heading to the city.

It has a big pool with water kept at moderate temperatues all the time.It has rich mineral supplies on the shores that can be applied before we immerse ourselves inside the water.There is a tiny waterfall and sauna room beside that.

You get some of the best captures if you have the camera carried all along during your swim.

Iceland Experience

Ensure you visit this place before you head to the city since its enroute.Avoid making half day trip from Reykjavik.

You don't more than 3 hours at this place.

Take your own towel to save 5 euros.

Buy waterproof case for camera/phone in order to capture yourselves when you are inside the water which I missed and realised during my swim around the pool.
