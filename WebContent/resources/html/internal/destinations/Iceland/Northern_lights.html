Date with Lady Aurora.

The most excited destination of all the trips was experiencing northern lights.I heard so much from my friend who had experienced this at Norway.

I ensured I do this on the day I land in Iceland since the possibility of missing it was high due to climatic conditions.The tour organisers had promised for rescheduling it again if we failed to witness this wonder.Usually go ahead for this tour is decided an hour before the actual tour starts depending on the prediction from the met department and experienced lads.

Finally there was a go ahead plan and I was picked up around 8 PM. Reach to the destination was around 10.30 PM.Northern lights come to limelight only around midnight/late night after it gets bright darker.The tour was comprised of 50 people travelling in a bus with high hopes and dreams.

We reached the spot around 10.30 PM and jumped onto the land for the impatient wait.It was hell cold outside and everyone was freezing.The guide was an experienced female and assured that we would be witnessing it.The stream was very thing and never became strong.We kept waiting for almost an hour before the gang headed back to the bus not withstanding the freezing temperature.

However , myself ,couple from America and the guide made up our mind to stay outside.We surmounted the cold to witness the stream finally gaining momentum.At a point we saw a meteor kind of thing the sky flashing and travelling at lighting speed.We really did not know what it was.

After sometime the stream grew more and more and we saw multiple shades of beauty in the sky.We started screaming inviting the ones waiting inside the bus.Finally , there arrived the moment when the lady aurora started dancing by travelling from one end to another.It was like a dream and we could not believe what we were witnessing.Our emotions were at peak at this moment.However , it was a shame that I missed capturing it in the camera since I did not own a tripod.

Iceland Experience

Ensure you fix slot on the very day you land in Iceland to see northern lights.

The reservation ticket is valid for 365 days and you will be able to make it again until you witness northern lights.

Do not go to witness without a tripod.Else , you will not be able to capture it.
