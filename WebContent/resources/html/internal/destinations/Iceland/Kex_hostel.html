Kex Hostel.

KEX was my first exposure to European hostel culture and I had no clue what it would look like.I had all butterfly thoughts as to how I will accomodate and adapt to the hostel environment.To my amazement , the experience was stupendous.I chose this place upon the recommendation of another friend.

Never new the greeting would be so warmer.Every turn you take you have an unknown face welcoming you.I had reserved for a 16 bed dormitory.The hostel has a inhouse bar serving drinks till 2 AM in the morning.The breakfast comes at a very cheap cost with tummy full of foods.The hostel is located just opposite to the harbor lake and gives you an aesthetic aerial view.

This was the place where I started cooking to save some money.The hostel facilities were outstanding where in one of the nights I had severe leg sprain which I aggravated from fall at Strokkur geysir spot that I had to disturb the receptionist at 2 AM in the morning.He was kind enough to get me a big ice bag to apply over the spot.

This was the place where I met fellow solo travellers from whom I shared and gained valuable experiences.

Iceland Experience
