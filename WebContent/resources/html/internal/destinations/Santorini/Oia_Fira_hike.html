Fira - Oia 10 KM hike.

The hiking trail activity was one of the best I did when I was in Santorini and a must do when you have sufficient time.The overall journey takes less than three hours provided you do not take too much pit stops for photos and give you some of the spectacular view of this hilly region that will leave you spell bounded.

I did this on a cloudy morning and was discouraged by many locals to refrain from doing owing to less fellow mates who would be taking it.But less sun shine indeed helps you to achieve the hike easily.Against all odds , with Umbrella ,headset,few snacks and a Water Bottle started my hike after parking my bike near Fira bus station.

The trail has main transit through Imerovigli and its the first pit stop that you will be taking during your hike.The entire trail is not paved though designated paths are there enroute which are smooth enough.Most of the inland route is through the villages located between Fira and Oia.The views are breath taking that you will not use see if you travel on road from Fria to Oia.

The entire trail path is signed and I did not find any difficulty in covering the route. Moving further , you would pass by Church of Agios Antonios (Saint Antony) and the Church of Prophet Elias

Now that you have almost reached Oia , you will start seeing the glimpse of this earthly beauty located just few KMs away.The entire walk was so refreshing and to my surprise it happened to be the St.George day ( falls during holy week of Easter). They were serving bread,potatoes,meat ,wine at the starting point of the town to everyone.
