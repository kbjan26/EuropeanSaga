Walk Around Oia.

After the bomb threat scene and delayed flight from Warsaw reached Athens around 2 A.M in the morning.I was supposed to take a ferry from Pireaus Port to Santorini and it was around 7 A.M in the morning. After much hassle reached one of the most exotic destinations in the world,Santorini.

The view from the port to Fira town is so captivating that you already get a feel of what is in stock.Directly headed to my accomodation to get refreshed to visit the tiny wonder ,heaven on Earth , Oia.

I hired a bike to travel across Santorini during my entire stay.I directly headed to Oia town around 2.30 P.M.You fall in love at the first place when you see the hill town from the entrance.You are just left with awe struck feeling to see such a stunning place on Earth.All the houses are built on top of mountain rocks and the view from them must be really amazing.

As soon as you enter the town ,you see many Asian couples thronging the place to take wedding shots captured.You just keep walking and walking across the corners to see which view is better but you will eventually find it hard to appreciate since every view will have aesthetic appeal.On my first visit , there was no sunset happening because of the climate and this gave me more space and time to roam around the town.I could even hike down the hills.The beauty of this place is highly elevated on the eve of the sunset.
