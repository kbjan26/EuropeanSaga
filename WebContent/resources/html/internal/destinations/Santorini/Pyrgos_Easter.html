Pyrgos Easter.

Pyrgos town is one of the best picturesque villages in Santorini.Located by few kilometres away ,the place is highly famous for its easter celebrations.It was included in my itinerary during my research around easter celebrations that are special in this island.I discovered the exact details of this event upon my return from Barcealona at Berlin SXF airport.The entire village is set ablaze from every roof top .every church,every path and supposed to be spectacular to view.I came to know that it is celebrated on Easter Friday.To my dismay , I had prebooked my flight from Athens to Santorini on Saturday morning.

My schedule went for toss after having realised that I had to be there to witness this spectacular event.I had to forego my flight costs and booked a speedboat from Athens to Santorini on the same day I land in Athens so that I would not miss this.Without any second thoughts I booked a boat service.

The entire people of the village gather on the eve of Easter on Friday around 9 PM gather at the main church to start the march or parade around the village.Religious prayers are uttered and candles are lit up before the start.Gradually the momentum increases and youngsters from the village start lighting up tiny candle like thing.As and when the march progresses , you see people gathered at various spots lit up making the view truly spectacular and the event lasts for couple of hours.The magical event is not to be missed when you are in Santorini.
