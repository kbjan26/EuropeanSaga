Oia sunset.

A spectacular event that elevates beauty of Oia.

The place is completely thronged by tourists to witness it.With shades of sun falling over this little town gives a complete different picture visually.There are many suggested spots to view it but the old Castle is the best of all.

I had been to Oia on two different days and missed out on Sun set on the first day due to bad weather but was fortunate enough to see it on the second day.Ensure you assemble at your viewing point atleast two to three hours before so that you grab a nice comfortable place to see this wonder happening at a relaxed pace.
