Light house cliffs.

The best of renting a bike in Santorini when you are all alone is to discover and cover new places that are not reached through public transport.One such place was the cliffs at light houe.This place is located on the farther West of Santorini Island near Akrotiri.

This place take quite some time from Akrotiri and it was suggested by fellow travellers from America whom I met on this island at two different occasions.The spot has number of cliffs and views of the island from here are simply stunning.A must place to visit when you are travelling at your own will in Santorini.
