Scuba Diving - Costa Brava - Spain.

One of the most cherished and celebrated events was scuba diving in Spain.After I started taking solo travelling seriously, I chose Spain as one of the destinations.I have seen the best of Spain from an Indian movie Zindagi na Milegi Dobara.The movie revolves around friends celebrating their life through travelling Spain completely.All the major events are filmed in that move one of which is Scuba diving.I ensured to do it when I am in Barcealona.

After doing enough research narrowed down on l'Estartit location in Costa Brava which are famous for diving schools.I chose a diving school which was offering photos as well for the overall price of 65 euros.Whole day was dedicated for this activity.

I chose it to do on a Saturday.Distance was pretty long from Barcealona.I had to take a train to a city called Girona and then had to take a bus to l'Estartit. The only service provider in this is AMPA and their time lines are very specific are very fixed but very reliable.

I had scheduled my diving at 10.30 AM.Started from Barcealona around 7.30 A.M by taking a train to Girona.After reaching Girona, the wait was around one hour for AMPA bus.It then started around 9 A.M and dropped me at 10 A.M.The guys from the diving school were waiting for me already for giving me a hell of an experience.

Initial settlement was around the size of the outfit and trying out the oxygen masks.Also instructed was the moves to be done when I am inside the works and to look out for the guide's signla to free the ear blockage.After finalizing on the equipments,the ride from the shore was around 15 mins where we went few kms inside where there are points identified for scuba divers of different categories.The adrenaline was gradually building then.I had imagined so many things and being an able swimmer assumed it be a cake walk.But what was in store was something different.

After reaching the spot,we started to suit ourselves with necessary outfits and equipments for the dive.Everything was normal until they added the oxygen cylinder and the weights around my waist.I felt so so heavy on top my weight and was asked to jump in to the water.

I could not understand what was happening initially since everything was out of control.I was there floating like a dead body unable to move my body and get the control.Then with Kevin's help, I went inside the water and was asked to breathe comfortably.I was breathing with heavy panic and could not sustain inside for more than a minute.This happened couple of times and felt like giving up by heart.But,Kevin and team were highly motivating and initial 10 minutes they helped me relax and bring stability.

Then,I started listening to the signals which had instructions clearly.After few meters inside , it was experience of a different feel seeing the wonder inside.We went 7 meters inside and the view was stunning.It was an unforgettable experience.I felt proud of doing it and one item was knoced off my bucket list finally.
