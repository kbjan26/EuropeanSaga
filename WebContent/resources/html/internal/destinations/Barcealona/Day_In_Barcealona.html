Barcealona City.

Berlin SXF airport has been my transit point for most of European destinations.Had a overnight lay in the airport since my flight was around 6.30 A.M.Reached Barcealona around 9 A.M.Realised the place is similar to Poland with many prefer speaking their own language after my struggle in buying train ticket.

Took renfe train from the airport and reached Plac Catalunya around 10.30.I had pre booked for one of the top attractions in the city , Sagrada Familia. The cost of the ticket is 29 euros and my slot was at 12.45 at the entrance and at 14.30 for one of the towers.I also ensure I booked my accomodation in city centre and locked in on Hostel 360 Barcealona.Sagrada Familia was just 20 mins walk from this place.

Constructions and main attractions inside the city are reflections of Gaudi's artistic plenitude.The same hangover is felt across the city where he has left us with his masterpieces that includes Casa Mila,Casa Batlo and Park Guel.

When I was there, April 2016 , there was construction still in progress at the top.For 29 euros you get audio guide,entrance to the church itself and tower top.Its better to book online in order to avoid long queues.

The architecture of this giant is simply stunnig and you will feel it even better when you are inside and witness the light rays that enter the building.Its amazing how he has visualised and constructed this beauty.

After visiting the the three main houses , headed to park guell by taking bus 24 from the city.The place is quite and I had prebooked my slot for 4 PM.Right from the entrance to the pavillion you find nuances of Gaudi's excellence.The place also has the Gaudi house Museum which was Gaudi's residence for almost twenty years.

I had reserved my late second half other day for La Rambles.After reading through many theories online , I perceived this place as one of the most unsafe areas to travel due to pick pocketting and petty crimes.

All my prejudice were shattered when I started roaming through the market and main street.This is one of the place with rich ancient history.It is also the place where you get traditional Catalunya food.Roamed and walked through the streets extensively before clocking in on one of the restaurants in the Market Square. It was the only place which was serving vegetarain version of payella and tapas.

One is not supposed to miss paella,tapas and Sagria which are traditional foods of Catalunya culture.They are delicious in deed.
