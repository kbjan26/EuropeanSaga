Barcelona vs Sporting gijon, Camp Nou.

What more can you ask for when you are in Barcealona.I was fortunate enough that I was able to witness a live Barca match. Yes , that right ,I watched them playing in Camp Nou stadium against Sporting Gijon.As part of trip research and travel planning realised there is a la liga match happening.Without any second thoughts booked a ticket and ensure I witness and feel the electrifying ,exuberance atmosphere.

It was a double dhamaka for me because , there were two events parts of the same day - Scuba diving at Costa Brava in the morning and late evening live match at Camp Nou.

I ensured I reach the place two and half hours before the game begins so that I see the building momentum in the ground.It was almost a sold house.There are many tourists like me ensure they do not miss on a live match when they are in Barcealona.Its a once in lifetime expereince for many including me.

Most of the fans get drunk enough before the match in the bars outside so that they are energized. There are many stalls available which sell accessories outside at a pretty decent price.I bought myself a scarf with the club name embedded in it for 10 euros.

Seats near the goal posts seem to be occupied by hardcore club fans and frequent visitors.They were carrying the momentum through out the match.It was like a dream witnessing Messi,Suarez,Neymar,Piquie and the rest playing.Barcealona cake walked through scoring 7-0.Experience of a lifetime indeed.

Barcealona Experience Barcealona Travel Planner

I had booked the ticket online way ahead for 85 euros.But to my surprise , the ticket was available inside the city shops even on the day of the match at 60 euros.If you are ready to take this risk avoid booking online and buy the tickets when you are in the city.
