Monsterrat - Spanish Monastery.

Monsterrat is one of the most soughted day trips from Barcealona.Did enough research around this place and chose this for its rich history.

Day 3 was exclusively allocated for this and the day ahead was very long.The whole cost of making it to the monastery is 29 euros.The cost includes 2 metro rides(you can use it to start from your accomodation and to return),train from Espanya to Monsterrat,cable car and funicular trains.

This website has every detailed information that will help you plan day trip to Monsterrat in an efficient way.

Jouney from Espanya is around an hour after which you reach this splendid beauty.Its located at hill top and the jouney to the top can be taken either using a train or cable car.I chose a cable car.The enroute experience and view using the cable car is simply breathtaking.Ensure you allocate whole day for this place since there are many TODO things.Monk's choir and further scaling top using funicular trains are must done things when you are in Monsterrat.

I stared my journey by visiting the museum which depicts the birthplace and the religious happenings.You will be taken away by the reason as to why this place considered to be sacred and also the dail routine of the monks living here who strive to hold the religious belief to the upcoming generations.

I roamed around the hill top to get some of the splendid views.The place also sells some of the best local cheese made of goat and cow milk.They are yummy and delicious.After couple of hours walk , sat to have my own lunch prepared by myself.

Also included in my itinerary was journey to further top using funicular trains.You never know a big hike is awaiting until you reach further top and the very reason why I allocated my entire day here.When you hike further you get some of the most amazing views and place to relax.

Once everything was done , I came to the monastery to witness boy's black choir as it is famously called. Its held twice a day , one at 12 PM noon and 6.45 in the evening.I missed the morning one and hence ensured I see it the evening before I leave.Its all together a different experience to small boys of the monastery and experienced monks come together to render their religious verses.
