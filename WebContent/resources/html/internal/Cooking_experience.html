Cooking experience during solo travelling.

Cooking is something that I have cultivated recently out of own interest and I am real proud of it.Since Indian by Nationality I am used to spicy food since childhood.Being a vegeterian I have found it hard and difficult to digest fries and salads all the time.But what triggered me cooking during my travels was completely different.





What did I do to rescue myself and how one can manage ?

  1. Carry a lunch box container.
  2. Carry basic spices and rice (one can even include some dhal) or you can be thankful to many of supermarkets in Europe who sell India spices in tiny containers.
  3. My list includes curry,jeera,turmeric and some home made masala powder.You can also carry some whole tamarind.
  4. Ensure hostel of choice has kitchenette facilities(I even call and get this confirmed at times.)
  5. Buy basic groceries of your choice that are required to prepare the dish.
  6. Ensure I make some nice Indian sambhar/curry so that one meal has Indian food.
  7. This is a huge cost saver as well.
  8. Have patience and sacrifice some sleep to get up early and prepare food.
